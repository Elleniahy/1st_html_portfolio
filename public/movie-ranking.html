Best Movies According to Ellen

My Great 3 Movies of All-time.

Avatar: The Last Airbender

Four elemental nations are at war with each other and only the Avatar, master of all four elements, can restore peace.

Grey's Anatomy

It's about the personal and professional lives of surgical interns, residents, and attendings at the fictional Seattle Grace Hospital.

Avengers

An all-star ensemble cast of established superhero characters from the Marvel Comics portfolio.
